India's Air Crisis
India is one of the global leaders of major pollution affecting millions of lives. In 2019, over 2.9 million people died a premature death due to pollution, and 1.6 million deaths from air pollution and overcrowding alone. The decline in household air and water pollution resulted in the increase of deaths due to industrial and chemical pollution. India is unnecessarily losing millions of their residents through the very air they rely on to breathe.
